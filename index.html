About Us

HerZone254 is a youth-led initiative focused on ending femicide and GBV through awareness, education, and empowerment. HerZone254 started with a question: What can we do to protect women and girls in our own communities? We're a group of young creators, coders, poets, and changemakers using our voices to stand against GBV and femicide.

  1. Providing Essential Services: Counseling, legal aid, and practical resources for survivors.
  2. Creating Safe Spaces: Secure environments for recovery and refuge.
  3. Advocating for Systemic Change: Fighting patriarchal norms and pushing for policy reform.

What is GBV & Femicide?

Learn about the different types of gender-based violence and how femicide affects women and girls across Kenya.

GBV is any harm done to someone based on their gender. In Kenya, this includes domestic violence, sexual assault, and online harassment. Femicide is the intentional killing of a woman because she is a woman.
